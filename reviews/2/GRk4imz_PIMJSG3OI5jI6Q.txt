Usually love traditional Mexican joints in non traditional neighborhoods. Impressed with the salsa & accompaniment bar but when my chicken torta arrived I was really disappointed. Boiled chicken on a dry bollio roll. Little flavor & messy. No mayo, avocado or any flavor binder. Bf had chicken taco- not good flour tortilla fried. Made for difficult biting. Enchiladas were good. Nice staff but until the food gets better we will be seeking out Roberto's
