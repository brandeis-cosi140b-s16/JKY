I'm so disappointed. My husband and I have heard such great things about this place, so we decided to try it. I don't know if it was because we ate there later in the day or what, but it wasn't good. I ordered the Pollo diablo with white rice. The chicken was super dry, and there wasn't really any onion to speak of. I think I got the last of what was left because 1/3 of it was just crunchy batter. My husband got the jerk chicken burro combo with sweet and sour chicken, rice and beans.
