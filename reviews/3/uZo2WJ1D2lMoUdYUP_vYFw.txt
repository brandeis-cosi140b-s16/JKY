Tried my first burrito and it was better than I expected- carnitas. I really liked the veggies in the burrito- broccoli, onion, red pepper. The hot sauce was very good too. I think the veggies are a California thing, but it works. The gaming area and bar is separate, as smoking is permitted there. They had a $10 slot credit for earning 10 points promo, which Golden Gaming also does.  Wahoo's has their own slot club, and I hope they do more promos and/or gaming events.
