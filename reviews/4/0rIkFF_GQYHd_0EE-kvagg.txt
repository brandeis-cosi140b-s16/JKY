This place is awesome and they are the only 24-hour burrito place I know of that serves quality cabeza.  Yes, you read that right--cabeza.  I've ordered cabeza burritos at numerous FBs and other bertos burrito shops but most times the quality is lacking and I find cartilage and small pieces of bone throughout my burrito--NOT AT ERIBERTOS!!!  These guys do cabeza right!  Even better, cabeza isn't even on their menu!  If you want to get rid of a hangover or avoid a hangover go to Eribertos and order yourself a cabeza burrito!
