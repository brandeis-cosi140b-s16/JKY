So you're walking through the mall by planet Hollywood and suddenly it hits you. You're starving. What should we eat? Oh, here's a quick taco joint, that'll probably be ok. If you walk down that path, you'll soon discover that Rockin' Taco is more than ok. 

For just under $10 you can get a few tasty tacos, a side, and a beer.  You pick your toppings as you move down the assembly line style ordering station, pay, and find a seat. 

The food is very tasty for the price point and you can decide to eat at rockin taco, order, pay, eat, drink your beer, and be back to walking the strip in under 30 minutes.
