Had lunch with the girls from work here today and it was delicious! I had a burrito with carne asada and all the fixin's just like I like it. It's like Subway in that respect, but edible, or Chipotle, but less rigid somehow. The chips and queso were alright and the iced tea was STRONG and tasty. 

The patrons were, shall we say, eclectic: from the unusual man in a business suit who danced his way out the door to "Kokomo" (which was playing at the time) to the grey skinned and skeletal heroin addict who combed the garbage in full sight of everyone. Eeeeep. Ah well, final memories of Tempe as I not so tearfully change jobs. Hello, NoSco...
