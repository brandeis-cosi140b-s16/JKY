They've got burritos, Mexican bowls, salads and tacos. All with your choice of meat (chicken, steak, carnitas, barbacoa, sofritas or veggie) with your options of cilantro-lime rice, pinto or black beans, a few salsas, cheese, sour cream included. Guacamole is available too, for $1.95extra. They also do chips & guacamole, chips and salsa. For kids, they put together a couple of little combo plate options. And for adults, they also have both beer and margaritas. Don't be afraid if the kind is out the door, it moves quickly and the food is good.
