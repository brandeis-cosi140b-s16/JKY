Don't need to be complicated. Just was here for the first time as I was on my way home  from work. This place is SUPER CLEAN and I tried 3 tacos. 1 Pastor, 1 Asada, and 1 Chicken.

The guy working told me it was only available on Sat and Sun. I will be back to try it as everything else was very good.
