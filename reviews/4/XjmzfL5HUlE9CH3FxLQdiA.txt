I have been wanted to check out this spot forever... So this day for lunch I entered the restaurant to a really friendly and helpful staff. I ordered the 3 taco combo It comes with rice and beans. The salsa was pretty good it could have been a bit hotter...I rate the food as followed.

Lenuga (Beef Tongue)  A
Barbacoa (Beef Cheek) B+
Carne Asada B
Beans C+
Rice C+

My lunch crew ordered an Al Pastor torta.. I was able to try this too super great flavor... I gave it an A+ I will be back again just to get that torta again along with great prices a must stop for anyone looking for some great food.
