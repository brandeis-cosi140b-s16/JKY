Cafe Rio is my new addiction. The food is delicious and affordable.  The pork is to die for.  Whether its the pork salad or pork tacos.  This location has outside seating option which is GREAT for days when the weather is nice.  The drink selections are AMAZING! Mint Limeade FAN.   

Be sure to get your loyalty punch card so that you can get a free meal after 10 purchases. :-)
