Very much enjoyed my dinner experience here. My favorite dish of the evening was, by far, the Duck Carnitas Empanada (duck confit, foie gras, orange habanero glaze, oregano, mushroom & queso Oaxaca). It's so rich and flavorful that you're going to have to order it, no matter what.

Crispy Pork & Cola Lime Glaze
