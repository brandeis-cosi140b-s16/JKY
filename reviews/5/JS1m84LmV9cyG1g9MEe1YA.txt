Super cute kitschy vintage retro vibe.  It's an Asian Mexican fusion and they pull it off flawlessly.  Service was great and we got each dish as it was ready - and I appreciate that.  Let me have my food when it's hot.  

The guacamole with toasted pumpkin seeds was delicious as was the tuna roll and the cookie ice cream sandwich.  I need to come back here when I'm not driving...
