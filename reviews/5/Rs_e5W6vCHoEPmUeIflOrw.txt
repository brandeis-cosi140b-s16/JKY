Chips and salsa before your meal: Yes
Self serve salsa bar: Yes
If you are looking for some daytime tacos this is the place to go. Tica tacos, carne asada, cochinita pibil, shrimp and even shark tacos. Vegetarian choices also available. If you love real horchata you'll love it even more here. They have the best horchata ever with fruit and crushed walnuts. This place is perfect because you can walk off the calories at Papago Park, Indian Steele Park or check out the sites downtown which are all within 10 minutes or less away.
