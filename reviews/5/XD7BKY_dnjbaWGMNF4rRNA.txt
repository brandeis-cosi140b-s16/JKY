Great food as expected, it's Chipotle! This location is perfect for the lunch time rush, as the staff is well prepared and equipped to keep the lunch time rush moving. Whether it's been getting a quick bite to eat or taking orders for co-workers on a lunch run, Chipotle gets the job done.
 
The clean environment, and the overall calm ambience compared to other Chipotle locations allows you to eat in peace or have a conversation without yelling across the table. Keep up the great work, and when in the area be sure to stop by and great one of the one/two item combinations, or build your own burrito. Don't forget the Chips and Salsa!
