Oh Little Some B!!!!! The first love of my life! 
Nothing has changed, since I was 4 years old. The grey trash cans, the brown plastic trays....the green awning....
LSB is a CG staple. I always get the ground beef taco (crisp) with extra cheese, side of beans, and a cheese crisp. It will rock your world! Take it from a CG native. You can't go wrong. The best Mexican food that ever was!
