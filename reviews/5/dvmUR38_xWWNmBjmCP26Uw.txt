Gorda's is a great taco place that empasizes on "baja." All the ingredients are always so fresh. They have a new soda machine with a lot of drink choices which is always good living in phoenix. The people who work there are good hardworking people and it's great to see them do what they are truly passionate about which is serving outstanding food with a well balance of their always fresh ingredients. Don't hesitate get down to Gorda's baja tacos and you won't be disappointed.
