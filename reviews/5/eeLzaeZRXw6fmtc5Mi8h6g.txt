One of our new favorite places! Fantastic tortas (current fave, barbacoa), great chips and flavorful salsa.  Knowing Spanish would definitely be a benefit here because not much of the menu is in English and the waitresses don't speak much English, but they're always pleasant and the food is yummy.  I think they must have grown since several of the earlier reviews; there's a side room now and table service; no placing the order at the window. Now if they'd just offer unsweet ice tea...
