OH MY GOD.

The first time you eat here, it will be a bit confusing, but they make the process of introduction easy and fun. You go up to the counter and try samples of everything, then make your order on a sheet. They bring it to your table along with one of their beloved Snickerdoodle cookies.

I've never become so addicted to anything in my life. The combination of fresh chinese and authentic mexican food is unreal. It's so good us Californians are begging our Arizona friends to Fed Ex this stuff back. The Red Jerk Chicken -THE BEST
