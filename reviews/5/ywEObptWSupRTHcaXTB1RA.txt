TACO SHACK.
HELL. EFFIN. YES.

$5 gets you two pork, beef or chicken tacos. $3 a taco for chorizo or fish. They've also always got a special of the day... but all these little taco delights are special. Special and effing delicious. These are the freshest tacos I've ever placed inside my mouth. My tastebuds were singing in delight- or maybe that was just me.
