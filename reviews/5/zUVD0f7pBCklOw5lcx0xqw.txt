Build Club was putting a structure on top of the roof of the hotel for an upcoming Art Event. After a long day of work we ordered food from the Gallo Blanco, the Hotel's in house Restaurant and boy were we not disappointed. The menu was a awesome mix of mexican and awesome. The Salsa and Guacamole were yummy, I had a Tuna Crudo that was spicy and orange with twist. They brought out all of our food right out to our cabana. great service and Great food! I'll be back soon!
