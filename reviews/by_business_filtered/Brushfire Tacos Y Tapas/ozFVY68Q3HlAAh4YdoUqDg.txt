If you're looking for a place that serves food like Taco Bell or Del Taco do not read any further!  Brushfire offers fresh quality food that is priced higher than fast food establishments, but cheaper than the sit down restaurants.  Do yourself a favor and check this place out if in the area or looking for some flavorful tacos and tapas.

Whether you walk up or go thru the drive thru, be prepared to enjoy some great Mexican/Spanish tapas. This place could be on anyone's weekly go to list without hesitation.
