The food is reasonably priced. Ordered the chicken quesadilla is a hearty size, the chicken moist, oozing with cheese, and tasted great. the shrimp ceviche wasn't really ceviche, but was excellent none the less. it was a mix of shrimp, avocados, tossed in a salsa. For those of you who worked out everyday for your Vegas trip, Gardunos offers a lo-carb menu. Hit this place before the pool party (and NOT Ditch Fridays) and your appetites should be satisfied and not feel bloated.
