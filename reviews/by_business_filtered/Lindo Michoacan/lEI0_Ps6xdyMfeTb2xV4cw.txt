Mexican food is probably my favorite and I really like Lindo.  I will admit that some things are a tad bland like the salsa and beans they serve with the chips but their entrees always make me happy!  I usually get either the chicken enchiladas or the bean and cheese burrito enchilada style smothered with sauce and cheese.  My boyfriend usually gets the veggie fajitas which are also pretty delicious.  The employees have always been pleasant and the place itself is in a nice area of town with awesome views of the strip
