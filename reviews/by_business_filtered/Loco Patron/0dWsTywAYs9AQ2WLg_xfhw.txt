Loco Patron is a must if you are looking for  authentic Mexican food and drinks. This resturant located in the historical downtown Scottsdale has a cozy welcome atmosphere. Indoor or outdoor patio dining. 
Our server Katherine F was excellent she, was very helpful she explained the menu and how the food is prepared. L had the lettuce tacos and watermelon Margarita, delicious!
