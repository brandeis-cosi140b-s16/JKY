The original Lindo Michoacan has been around for a long time, and they have Mexican food down. Considering the owner is from Mexico and his soon attended culinary school, this restaurant has an amazing variety of Mexicans dishes and a service that never fails. Visit this place if your looking for authentic Mexican food and enjoy The love mariachi! The mariachi is amazing and the food is the best!
Mariachi is there Friday and Saturday from 7:30-9:30.
