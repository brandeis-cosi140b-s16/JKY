Came here on a whim, on a stop-over for a flight to Hawaii. The front desk guy told me about this place, saying that it was worth the cab ride out there. And boy was it! The rice was so fluffy and paired with the Back Beans it was perfect. I ordered some Enchiladas and the Pollo Asada and washed it down with 2 slamming, potent Margarita's! But I punked out at the end of the meal and ordered the Gringo tourist special-Fried Ice Cream. Such is life.....
