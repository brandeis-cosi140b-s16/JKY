There are countless Chipotle-esque fusion restaurants, but this Hawaiian concept works pretty well! The meats (I had the steak and chicken) are tasty and the available choice of toppings work well together.

As mentioned a million times on here, the Coconut Bark is great if caramel, chocolate and coconut are your thing.

Not much to it, you can build it fairly healthy or as unhealthy as you want.
