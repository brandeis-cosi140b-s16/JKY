A slice of Mexico right on the Las Vegas strip. Originally from Tijuana, this taco joint is not for the faint hearted. Choice of meat includes beef tongue, beef shoulder and pork all freshly prepared and resting on delicious tortillas under a generous helping of fresh cilantro, onions and peppers. And you thought Tijuana was oNly for cheap booze and raunchy bars.
When in Vegas stop here and your taco cravings will be satisfied.
Viva Mexico!
