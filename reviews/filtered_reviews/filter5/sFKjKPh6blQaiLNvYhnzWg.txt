This is the corner where business comes to die, but I hope this one makes it. 

As soon as the pedestrian white walls of Ye Olde Donut & BBQ and, more recently, the late Derald's Diner were washed in the much richer brick red paint, the folks across the street at El Charro got off their asses and re-painted their "Tortas - Tacos - Burritos" signs. American capitalism at work.

Seating is limited at Maize and there are no public bathrooms. It is counter service with about ten barstools.
