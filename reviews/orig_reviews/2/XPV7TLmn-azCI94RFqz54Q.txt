The TeePee is a mystery, Of what you ask? The mystery is, Why do people still come here?

The food is average, the margaritas are average, the service is average. The only thing I can say they excel at is the noise level.

There are a hell of a lot better places to get Mexican in Phoenix. I will forgive you if you chose this place over Macayo's or Garcia's, it is a step up from there.
