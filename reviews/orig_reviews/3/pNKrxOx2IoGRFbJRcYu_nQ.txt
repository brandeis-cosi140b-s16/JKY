Seems that the entree's are about $5 more than you'd pay elsewhere, but you're paying extra for the atmosphere.  However, if you plan on eating inside, be prepared that the noise level may be quite loud, as it echoes.

You never know when you'll find yourself sitting akin to a 'girls night out' table, with several shrill voices.

Their flavorful pork verde will bring us back, but next time we'll wait for the patio.
