You can smell the aroma emanating from the grill in the parking lot . . . now that's effective advertising. The arrepas are que bueno; go to the salsa bar and add fresh pico and hot salsa to pull all the flavor together. Order two and that's your meal. The burritos are too damn big and will fall apart after the first few bites. Share. You will definitely get street grill flavor at street grill prices.
