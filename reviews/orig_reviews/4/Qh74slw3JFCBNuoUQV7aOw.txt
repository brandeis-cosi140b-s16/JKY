Amidst the abundance of pawn shops, auto repairs and payday loan storefronts lies the haven that is Los Dos Molinos.  If you feel that you have entered a garage sale/thrift store, you have found your refuge.  Your synapses will be overloaded as you take in the random yet tasteful decorum.  The classy plastic Statue of Liberty tugs at your patriotic heartstrings and the tinsel covered atrium makes everyday feel like Cinco de Mayo. 
Simply smelling the tantalizing food was almost satisfying enough, which was perfect because I was only interested in the margaritas.  Liquid diet.  Prickly pear pitcher of margaritas is a steal of a deal and was empty all too soon.
