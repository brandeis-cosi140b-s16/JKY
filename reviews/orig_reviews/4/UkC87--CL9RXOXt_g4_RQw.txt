Mesa suffers from a serious poverty of fun things to do and good places to eat, but before you start praying for daylight there is hope! If you're tired of soulless chain establishments and Mexican food that seems a little too authentic, but don't want to flee to Tempe or Phoenix Tia Rosa's is just what the foodie ordered.

Salmon Taco with Mango Salsa, need I say more? Yum - E!
