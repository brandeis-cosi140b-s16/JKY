Holy Tequila!  Luv the margaritas here!  House margaritas are the best.  I highly recommend the Cucumber margarita!  Its cool and refreshing with the perfect amount of tequila.  Great drink for the hot AZ summer!  Tinga tacos are my absolutely favorite.  Great crunchy chicken taco!  Great place in OT for some awesome food and drinks.  This is one of the best happy hour locations in town!
