Had drinks and appetizers at Loco Patron tonight. The patio is always a fun place to sit and people watch, munch on the pork tacos, and have a few beers. Always a good crowd, so you never feel alone at the bar or the patio seating. Loco does a great job of serving an eclectic mix of people, young and old, casual and a bit dressed up. Try the shrimp tacos or fajitas for a special treat with a cold beer. Plan to sit outside--it's the place to be
