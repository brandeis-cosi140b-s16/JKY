Tucked away inside a charming little house off the hustle and bustle of 7th Avenue is this gem that cooks up some of the best shrimp burritos around. They're refreshingly light, a welcome change after all the sauce-smothered enchilada style and heavy carne asada burros I've been scarfing down on my PHX-Mex tour. 

Oh, they make a mean carne asada too...
