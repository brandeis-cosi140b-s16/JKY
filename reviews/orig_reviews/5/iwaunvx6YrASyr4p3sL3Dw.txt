We loved this place so much we went back twice over our one-week vacation in the Phoenix area!  If not for Yelp, we never would have walked in.  It's tucked away in an old strip mall, and the ambiance leaves a bit to be desired, but the food is amazing!  We especially loved the Brule burger and the French toast fries, but everything we tried was delicious.  If only we lived a bit closer...
